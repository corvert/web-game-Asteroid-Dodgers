Controls

↑ or W Move Forward
↓ or S Break/Slow Down
← or A Turn Left
→ or D Turn Right
ESC Pause/Resume

Objective

  • Navigate your spaceship through the asteroid field
  • Avoid colliding with asteroids to stay alive
  • Survive longer than other players to win
  • The last player standing wins the round!

Tips

  • Stay alert - asteroids move in unpredictable patterns
  • Use smooth movements to navigate tight spaces
  • Watch other players' positions for strategic advantages
  • Don't panic - steady movements often work better than quick jerks
  • Collect powerups to gain temporary advantages
  • Your ship will stop at the screen borders - plan your movements accordingly

Powerups

Shield 5 seconds of invulnerability to asteroids
Speed Boost 50% faster movement for 5 seconds
Bonus Points Instantly gain 50 bonus points

Multiplayer

  • Create a room or join an existing one
  • Share the room code with friends
  • Wait for all players to join before starting
  • Enjoy competing against friends in real-time!
